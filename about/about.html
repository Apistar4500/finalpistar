About Us

GTBT was founded in Legazpi in 2019 and is named after the the fan we had. Initially only around 20 pairs of shirts a day were created, each one caringly hand-painted logo. GTBT captured the imagination of the detail-obsessed lokal fashion crowd and spurred a revial of interest in vintage shirts which spread around the world. Today GTBT has gained critical acclaim, as one of the ultimate shirt must haves, is internationally know and embraced by both collectors and hipsters alike. GTBT was initially more a labour of love than a commercial venture and despite phenomenal success GTBT mission to create the highest quality products has remained unchanged. This underlying value defines GTBTs position as an icon in the history of denim wear.
